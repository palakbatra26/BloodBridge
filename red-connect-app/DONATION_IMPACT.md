# Donation Impact Information

## How Your Donation Helps Save Lives

Every contribution to BloodBridge directly supports our mission of connecting blood donors with recipients in real-time. Here's exactly how your donation makes a difference:

### Impact by Amount

| Amount | Impact | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| ₹50 | Refreshments for 5 donors | Provides snacks and beverages for donors after donation |
| ₹100 | Emergency transportation support | Helps cover transportation costs for urgent blood requests |
| ₹500 | Small blood camp organization | Covers basic costs for organizing a local blood donation camp |
| ₹1000 | Platform maintenance for 1 month | Supports our technology platform that connects donors and recipients |

### Fund Distribution

We maintain complete transparency in how donations are used:

1. **Blood Camp Organization (40%)**
   - Venue booking and setup
   - Medical supplies and equipment
   - Staff coordination
   - Promotional materials

2. **Technology Platform (30%)**
   - Server hosting and maintenance
   - Software development and updates
   - Security and data protection
   - User support and troubleshooting

3. **Donor Support & Refreshments (20%)**
   - Refreshments for donors
   - Recognition and reward programs
   - Donor appreciation events
   - Educational materials

4. **Emergency Transportation (10%)**
   - Urgent blood delivery coordination
   - Transportation for critical cases
   - Emergency response support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is my donation tax deductible?
A: Donations may be eligible for tax deductions. Please consult with a tax professional for guidance specific to your situation.

### Q: How can I track where my donation went?
A: You'll receive an automatic receipt with your payment details. For donations above ₹1000, we can provide a detailed impact report.

### Q: Can I specify how my donation is used?
A: Yes, you can mention your preference during donation. We'll do our best to allocate funds accordingly while maintaining operational flexibility.

### Q: Are there recurring donation options?
A: Currently, we support one-time donations. Recurring donation options will be available in a future update.

### Q: How do I get a donation receipt?
A: An automatic PDF receipt is generated and downloaded after every successful payment.
